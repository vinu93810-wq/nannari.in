How to Consume Nannari Sharbat

1
Mix 2-3 tablespoons of Nannari sharbat in a glass of cold water (200-250ml).
2
Stir well and add ice cubes for a refreshing summer drink.
3
Consume 1-2 glasses daily for best results, especially during hot weather.
4
Can also be added to milk, smoothies, or used as a natural sweetener in desserts.
