Refund & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Please review our policies regarding returns, damages, and important usage instructions.

Important Usage Instruction

Nannari Sharbat is highly concentrated and sensitive to direct water contact. If even a single drop of water enters the bottle, the product quality may be affected.

  • Do not add water directly into the bottle.
  • Always take the required quantity using a dry spoon and mix it separately with water or milk in a glass.

Damaged on Arrival?

If you receive a damaged or leaking Nannari bottle at delivery:

  • Contact us within 24 hours
  • Share clear photos/videos of:
    • The damaged bottle
    • Outer packaging
    • Shipping label
  • Once inspected and approved, a replacement will be dispatched within 3–5 business days.

Refunds (If Applicable)

  • Refunds are issued only if a replacement is not possible.
  • Processed within 3 business days after approval.
  • Amount credited to original payment method within 5–7 business days.
